Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Linkdln Jobseekers ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your data. This policy explains how our Chrome Extension operates and handles your information.

By using Linkdln Jobseekers, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Linkdln Jobseekers is a client-side extension that operates entirely within your browser. All data extracted or processed by the extension remains on your local device.

  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not collect your IP address or device information.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Usage

Any data processed by the extension is used strictly for the functionality you initiate. Specifically:

  • Job & User Information: Data extracted from pages you visit is displayed to you within the extension popup and stored locally for your convenience.
  • Gmail Integration: When you choose to send an email, the extension opens a Gmail compose window using a prefilled URL (e.g., https://mail.google.com/...). We do not have access to your Gmail account, credentials, or inbox contents. We simply facilitate opening the draft window with the recipient's information you provide.

Data Storage

All data generated or saved by Linkdln Jobseekers is stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local environment.

This data is sandboxed and is not accessible by us or any other websites you visit. You have full control over this data and can clear it at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser's extension data.

Third-Party Services

Our extension acts as a standalone tool and does not rely on backend servers or third-party analytics services.

However, the extension allows you to interact with third-party platforms (like Gmail or LinkedIn) directly via your browser. Interactions with these platforms are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Permissions Explanation

To function correctly, Linkdln Jobseekers requires specific permissions:

  • "activeTab": To read the content of the current tab when you activate the extension, allowing us to extract relevant job or user details.
  • "storage": To save your preferences and extracted data locally on your device.
  • "scripting": To execute scripts necessary for data extraction on the pages you visit.

We request the minimum permissions necessary to provide the extension's core features.

User Control & Consent

You retain full ownership of your data.

  • Access: You can view all stored data within the extension's interface.
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by removing the extension from Chrome.
  • Opt-out: since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no need to opt-out of tracking or marketing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The latest version will always be available on this page.
