About Instaclean

Instaclean is a FREE email organizer that deletes & blocks spam emails and organizes your inbox in more efficient way. It is the best email cleaner tool for Gmail, Hotmail, Outlook & Yahoo Mail. With the help of Instaclean, you can bulk delete junk emails and block them. It is a completely free inbox cleaner app for your mailbox which lets you take full control of your inbox and help to regain it back.

Although Gmail has a good anti-spam filter, spammers always manage to land suspicious emails in your inbox. Here Instaclean plays its role.

Company: Vumonic Datalabs - Bangalore

Role Note: I worked on the mobile app of Instaclean. A different team member created the website.

Features

Instaclean is free for a lifetime with No advertisements and no charges! Main features include:

  • Unsubscribe: Unsubscribe from spam mailing lists effortlessly.
  • Bulk Delete: Delete junk and unwanted emails within just a click.
  • Organization: Organize your inbox in a more efficient way.
  • Clutter Free: Clean all the clutter from mailing lists that you do not follow anymore.

Instaclean & Environment

One email generates 10g of CO2 emission per year and 200 million emails are sent every minute. 2,000 tons of CO2 is emitted every minute from emails only! Cleaning up your inbox is an easy way to reduce your carbon footprint and save the environment.
