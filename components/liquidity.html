About

Welcome to Liquidity.io – your one-stop solution for managing private stocks, SBA7 trades, and digital assets! Whether you're an experienced investor or just getting started, Liquidity.io provides you with all the tools you need to trade, track, and manage your portfolio with ease.

Liquidity.io leverages blockchain technology to bring unprecedented liquidity to the alternative investment market. Our platform features a no-code API-minting solution, enabling users to swiftly digitize portfolios into tradeable securities. With a focus on security and transparency, Liquidity.io is reshaping how real-world assets are traded.

Key Features

  • Seamless Onboarding: Hassle-free experience with phone verification and OTP.
  • Trade Private Stocks & SBA7: Manage diverse investments in one place.
  • Portfolio Management: View performance, manage orders, and build strategies.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Easy access to Watchlist, Portfolio, Trade, and more.
  • Digital Assets: Securely store and track minted assets and crypto.
  • Blockchain-Powered Security: Immutable transaction records for transparency.
  • Real-time Updates: Instant price updates, WAC, and basis points monitoring.

Key Benefits

  • For Investors: Better price discovery, reduced fees, and increased liquidity.
  • For Issuers: Access to extensive distribution channels and lower capital formation costs.
  • For Investment Banks/Brokers: Continuous commissions and sustainable revenue streams.
